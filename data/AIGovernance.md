# Apex Digital Solutions AI Governance Policy

Document Owner: AI Governance Board

## Transparency

AI-generated recommendations must include supporting evidence.

AI-generated outputs should identify the source of information when available.

## Human Oversight

Business stakeholders must review AI-generated recommendations before execution.

## Responsible AI

AI systems must not expose confidential customer information.

AI systems should provide explainability whenever possible.

## Risk Management

AI solutions impacting customers require governance review before production deployment.
