# Apex Digital Solutions Release Process

Document Owner: Engineering Operations

## Deployment Requirements

Before production deployment, the following approvals are required:

- Product Owner Approval
- Security Approval
- Architecture Approval

## High Risk Releases

High risk releases require:

- Rollback plan
- Deployment checklist
- Stakeholder notification

## Release Windows

Production deployments must occur during approved deployment windows.

Emergency releases require Engineering Director approval.
